  • About Rarc
Rarc Was born in the year 1964 in Denver, Colorado. Growing up, Rarc was inspired from his dad Stan Brakhage, who was a respected

film maker. Rarc tried other forms of art like photography, but what really stuck was woodworking. For years he has been making interior

wood work design for many companies, big and small. He couldn't do it alone though. Rarc makes all this with the help of a small team of

about five people(they come and go), and occasionally, when no one else is there, his family helps too.
